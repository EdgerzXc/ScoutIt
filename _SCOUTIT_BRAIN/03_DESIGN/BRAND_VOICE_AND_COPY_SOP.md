# Brand Voice & Copy SOP

> How every word on ScoutIt should read. Use this before writing or editing ANY user-facing copy —
> headings, sublines, buttons, tooltips, descriptions, error messages, emails. Established 2026-06-26
> during the brand-voice overhaul. Pairs with `00_SOP.md` and the `Brand / Visual Guardian` +
> `Brand & Voice` council seats.

---

## 1. The messaging spine (say this, everywhere, consistently)

> **ScoutIt is the Philippines' first spatial commerce platform — a B2B2C intelligence layer for
> every kind of space. Not a listings site. Not a brokerage. The decision layer that sits above both.**

Three jobs that one sentence does: claims the category, states the model, says what we're *not*.

- **Category:** spatial commerce (Bloomberg for space). *Never* "e-commerce" (see §5).
- **Model:** B2B2C — we serve businesses (owners, brokers, photographers, researchers, planners) who
  reach consumers (seekers/buyers/renters). We are the platform in the middle, not a broker ourselves.
- **The promise:** *Real signals. No fake listings. No pressure.*
- **The feeling:** every space — small, grand, humble, rare — is welcome and shown at its best. We
  bring the *wonder* back to finding space.

---

## 2. Voice principles

1. **Clear beats clever.** If a line needs a second read, cut it. Plain words win.
2. **Warm, not corporate.** We sound like the layer pages ("We're the deep archive holding you up"),
   never like a hedge-fund deck.
3. **Confident, not boastful.** State what we do plainly; let the difference speak.
4. **Short.** Headlines are a few words. Sublines are one sentence. Scroll beats are a breath.
5. **Premium through restraint.** Black-heavy, gold once per beat. Same discipline in words: one
   strong idea per line, not three.
6. **Human + a little cosmic.** The cosmic metaphor is seasoning (Orbit, descent, the void), never
   the whole meal. Never let it get cute or hide the meaning.
7. **Inclusive.** A townhouse in Cavite matters as much as a tower in BGC. Say so.

---

## 3. Always surface these differentiators (in headlines, not footnotes)

- **No fake listings / real signals** — the direct answer to the Lamudi/Carousell trust problem.
- **Intelligence first, no pressure** — you never pay to look; we never pressure-sell.
- **First spatial commerce platform / B2B2C** — the category claim.
- **Every space welcome, shown at its best** — the inspiration + inclusivity thread.

---

## 4. Banned words & phrases (the jargon we removed — do not bring back)

Replace MBA/finance-deck language with plain human words:

| Banned | Use instead |
|---|---|
| "engineering the acquisition and transfer of assets" | "helps people choose space with confidence" |
| "information asymmetry dictates value" | "the market is noisy and full of half-truths" |
| "high-fidelity matrix", "institutional-grade" | "one clear, verified briefing" |
| "serious capital deployment", "deploying inventory" | "deciding on a space", "listing a space" |
| "Asset Liquidity", "Commissioned Deployment" | "the right match", "work that counts" |
| "Redefining Capital Placement in Emerging Asia" | "Why this matters now." |
| "next-generation", "cutting-edge", "revolutionary" | (cut — show, don't claim) |
| "Asset Holders", "Service Elite" | "Space Owners", "The Professionals" |

General: no "next-gen", no empty hype adjectives, no Title Case headlines, no ALL-CAPS sentences
(short mono eyebrows may be uppercase). Sentence case for everything else.

---

## 5. Compliance-safe wording (RA 9646 — hard rule)

- The platform's stance is **"Intelligence First. Transactions Never."** We display and inform; we
  never facilitate the sale.
- Therefore: **say "spatial commerce", never "spatial e-commerce."** "E-commerce" implies an
  on-platform checkout we deliberately do not have. The *commerce* we run is discovery, intelligence,
  and connection (the Connects economy + the handshake) — not the property sale.
- Prices: copy never implies ScoutIt sets or brokers a price. A price is **the owner's asking price**,
  shown only in the property "Your Move" section, owner-verified. No price data → say nothing.

## 6. Reference: the locked rewrites (before → after)

The canonical examples of the voice. Match these.

- **Footer (site-wide):** ~~"Next-generation space intelligence and verification platform for
  high-value Philippine properties."~~ → "The Philippines' first spatial commerce platform. Every
  kind of space — homes, offices, venues, tables — decoded into clear, verified intelligence."
- **Hero value line:** ~~"A premium real estate platform for founders, creatives, and enterprises…"~~
  → "The Philippines' first spatial commerce platform. We turn every kind of space… into clear,
  verified intelligence. No fake listings. No pressure. Just the signals that matter."
- **About lead:** ~~"We are a space intelligence platform engineering the acquisition and transfer of
  prime real estate assets…"~~ → "ScoutIt isn't a listing site. We're the Philippines' first spatial
  commerce platform — the intelligence layer that helps people read, understand, and choose space
  with confidence."
- **About pillars:** ~~The Matrix / The Advisors / The Architecture~~ → The Intelligence / The People /
  The Experience.
- **Pricing intro:** ~~"ScoutIt monetizes access, intelligence, visibility, and connection…"~~ → "We
  charge for access, intelligence, and connection — never for listings, and never for looking."

## 7. The checklist (run before shipping any copy)

- [ ] Does it match the spine (§1) — category, model, promise?
- [ ] Plain enough that a busy non-expert gets it in one read?
- [ ] Warm, not corporate? No banned jargon (§4)?
- [ ] Sentence case, short, one idea per line?
- [ ] A differentiator surfaced where it counts (§3)?
- [ ] Compliance-safe wording (§5) — "spatial commerce", price stance honest?
- [ ] Reads like the rest of the site (consistent), not a one-off?
