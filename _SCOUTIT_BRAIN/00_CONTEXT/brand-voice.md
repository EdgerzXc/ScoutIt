# ScoutIt Brand Voice & Style Guide

## Overview
ScoutIt is a premium, ultra-luxury commercial and residential real estate directory in the Philippines. Our tone must reflect exclusivity, high-end quality, and utmost professionalism. We cater to high-net-worth individuals, top-tier brokers, and enterprise clients.

## The "White-Glove" Luxury Tone
- **Professional, yet approachable:** Use refined, sophisticated vocabulary without sounding overly complex or archaic.
- **Cinematic & Evocative:** Describe spaces in a way that evokes emotion. Use words like *curated, bespoke, panoramic, seamless, prestige, cornerstone, uncompromising*.
- **Authoritative & Trustworthy:** We provide definitive intelligence. Avoid tentative language ("might be", "we think"). Use strong, confident assertions.
- **Concise & Impactful:** Avoid fluff. Every word should add value or atmosphere.
- **Formatting:** Use structured formats (short paragraphs, clear bullets) to respect the reader's time.

## SEO Integration
When generating property descriptions or marketing copy:
1. **Never sacrifice tone for SEO.** Keywords must be embedded naturally and seamlessly.
2. **Highlight prestige:** If a property is in "BGC, Taguig", frame it as "Located in the prestigious enclave of Bonifacio Global City".
3. **Focus on key luxury amenities:** Always highlight unique selling points (e.g., private elevators, Grade-A certification, LEED status, unobstructed skyline views).

## Example Transformation
**Bad (Generic/Raw):**
"50sqm office for rent in BGC. Good location near malls. 50k per month."

**Good (ScoutIt White-Glove SEO):**
"Positioned in the heart of Bonifacio Global City, this premier 50-sqm commercial space offers an unparalleled opportunity for growing enterprises. Surrounded by high-end retail hubs and lifestyle destinations, this bespoke office delivers both strategic connectivity and elevated corporate prestige."
