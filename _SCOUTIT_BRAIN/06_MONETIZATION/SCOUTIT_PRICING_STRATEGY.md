# ScoutIT — Pricing & Subscription Strategy
## Internal Document · Not for Public Release · Last Updated: June 2026

> This document is the definitive internal reference for ScoutIT's subscription architecture, tier logic, Connects economy, and go-to-market sequencing. It is not a public pricing page. Prices are placeholder until validated with real users.

## The Core Philosophy

ScoutIT monetizes **access, intelligence, visibility, and connection** — not listings.

We never charge for the act of discovering a space. We charge for how deep you go, how visible you become, and how protected your identity is while doing it.

Every user type has a free tier that is genuinely useful — not crippled. Cold-start growth depends on this. Nobody hits a wall on day one.

## The Connects Economy

**Connects** are ScoutIT's internal transaction currency. They power every action that crosses user boundaries — outreach, pitching, hiring, requesting.

### Distribution Rules
- Every tier receives a monthly Connect allocation (resets monthly, does not roll over)
- Additional Connects can be purchased à la carte at any tier
- Connects earned via bounty task completions **stack on top** of monthly allocation and **do not expire**
- Starry users can earn unlimited Connects through bounties — this is intentional

### Monthly Connect Allocations by Tier
| Tier | Seekers | Brokers | Owners | Photographers | Researchers |
|------|---------|---------|--------|---------------|-------------|
| Starry | 1 | 1 | 1 | 1 | 1 + bounty |
| Solar | 6 | 8 | 6 | 5 | 5 + bounty |
| Cluster | 15 | 20 | 18 | 12 | 12 + bounty |
| Universe | 40 | 50 | 40 | 25 | 25 + bounty |

### What Connects Are Used For
- Seeker → Broker: initiate anonymous or direct contact
- Broker → Owner: pitch for listing representation rights
- Owner → Photographer: commission a shoot
- Owner → Researcher: commission a field report
- Owner → Event Planner: commission a space design
- Researcher: propose a new bounty task (Universe tier only)

## The Bounty System

Bounties are ScoutIT's crowdsourced data engine. They solve the data ingestion bottleneck without payroll.

### How a Bounty Works
1. **Creation** — Platform, Universe Seekers, or Universe Data Principals flag a data gap (e.g. "verify quiet level score for Aurelia Unit X")
2. **Claim** — Any researcher (any tier) claims the task from the bounty dashboard
3. **Submission** — Researcher uploads geo-tagged, time-stamped proof
4. **Verification** — AI parsing layer validates against existing data, flagged for human review
5. **Payout** — +1 Connect awarded. Property page updates to "Verified by ScoutIT Community"
6. **Owner approval** — For Cluster+ owners, bounty data on their property requires their approval before going live

## The ID Card System

A downloadable, ScoutIT-branded credential available to paid service providers and brokers.

**Who gets it:** Solar tier and above for Brokers, Photographers, and Researchers.

**What it contains:**
- Name and profile photo
- ScoutIT tier badge
- Verification status
- QR code linking to live ScoutIT profile
- Expiry date (tied to active subscription)

**How it is used:**
- Brokers: show to buyers and owners as a trust signal
- Photographers: present on-site to property owners before a shoot
- Researchers: carry for field access and owner cooperation

**Technical note:** ID card auto-expires when subscription lapses. QR code always points to live profile — if subscription is inactive, profile shows "Inactive" status.

## Go-To-Market Sequencing

### Do NOT launch the pricing page until:
- [ ] First 5 real brokers onboarded and active
- [ ] Connects system is functional (something to spend them on)
- [ ] Onboarding flow routes users into the correct tier by role
- [ ] At least 20 real property listings live

### What to do before that:
- Save this document as internal north star for all build decisions
- Use tier feature logic to decide what goes behind which paywall in the codebase
- Build a "Coming Soon" waitlist page using only the cosmic tier names — no prices, no feature lists

### Pricing validation approach:
- **The B2C vs B2B Pivot:** Seekers are priced for massive volume (₱149/mo, ₱499/mo). Brokers/Owners are priced as high-value B2B SaaS.
- **The Early Adopter (Founding Member) Strategy:** To overcome the cold-start problem (zero network effect), display the real B2B premium prices crossed out, and offer a massively discounted "Founding Member" price for the first 100 Supply-Side users.
  - Broker Cluster Strategist: <del>₱6,499</del> → ₱1,999/mo
  - Owner Cluster Developer: <del>₱7,999</del> → ₱2,499/mo
- Never publish prices on the site without this psychological anchoring during the bootstrapping phase.
